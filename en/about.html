3NC & The Laboratory

The Manufacturer

Precise Additive Manufacturing in the heart of Dresden.

The Philosophy

From Maker to Industrial Manufacturer.

3NC stands for uncompromising quality in additive manufacturing. From a fascination with materials science and complex geometries, a specialized laboratory for demanding prototyping and small series production was born here in Dresden.

We don't just print plastic. We analyze load cases, select the exact right polymer for your specific application, and, if required, electroplate the surfaces with real metal. Our goal: Components that can withstand industrial use.

Competencies

Machines and Expertise

FDM Technology

Industrial printers with heated chambers for warp-free carbon fiber and ABS prints.

SLA Technology

High-resolution resin printing for fits in the micro-tolerance range (0.05mm).

Materials Science

Deep understanding of thermal, mechanical, and chemical load limits of polymers.

Electroplating

In-house electroplating for finishing printable plastics with real metal layers.

Design for Additive

Optimization of CAD models for tool-less manufacturing to reduce cost and weight.

Small Batch Logistics

Reliable production and quality control (Bridge-Manufacturing) up to 500 pieces.
