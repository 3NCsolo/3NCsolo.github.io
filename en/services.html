Thermoplastic Extrusion

FDM High-Performance Printing

We produce stable mechanical parts, spares, and prototypes on industrial FDM machines. With heated build chambers, we can reliably process dimensionally stable technical polymers.

Our Materials:

  • Standard & Tough: PLA Elite, PETG (Food-safe).
  • High Performance: ABS, ASA (UV-resistant), PC-Blend.
  • Specialty Tribology: Carbon-Nylon (PA-CF), Igus gliding filaments, TPU.

Stereolithography

SLA Resin Printing

For microscopic details, housings with precise fits, or jewelry masters. SLA technology provides absolutely smooth surfaces and layer-free optics.

Post-Processing

Smoothing & Electroplating

A 3D print doesn't have to look like plastic. We offer vapor smoothing to make FDM parts waterproof and high-gloss sealed.

Metal on Plastic:

Our core competency: We coat 3D printed parts (ABS/Resin) in our own plating facility with real various real metals. Perfect for prototypes, EMI shielding, or exclusive single pieces.

Feasibility Studies & Custom Solutions

Smart Devices & IoT

Do you have an idea for a smart product but can't find anything off-the-shelf? We transfer our Smart Home expertise to individual hardware solutions.

From PCB to Housing:

Whether it's a fully wired smart door sill, custom-made sensor housings, or standalone IoT gadgets: We test technical feasibility, design the housing (CAD), and integrate the electronics into a functional prototype.

Via our established partner network, we realize high-precision CNC parts made of various high-strength industrial metals. We adapt your design data for machining processes and handle the complete quality management.
