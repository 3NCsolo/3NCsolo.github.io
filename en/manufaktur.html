A 3D print doesn't have to look like a 3D print. Through acetone vapor smoothing, we achieve injection-mold-like surfaces. Our highlight: The galvanic metal coating of plastics with copper, nickel, and gold for true metallic feel and conductivity.

Development & Scaling

From Prototype to CNC Series

We cover the entire product lifecycle: From the first custom build (prototyping) to additive short-runs and transition to industrial manufacturing. Once the 3D-printed proof of concept is established, we can also manufacture your part via our established industrial network through machining (CNC milling / turning) from high-strength metals such as various high-strength industrial metals.
