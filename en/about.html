The Manufactory

Engineering-driven Additive Manufacturing in the heart of Dresden.

The Philosophy

From Maker to Industrial Manufacturer.

3NC stands for uncompromising quality in additive manufacturing. Born from a fascination with materials science and complex geometries, we established a specialized laboratory in Dresden for demanding prototyping and short-runs.

We don't just print plastic. We analyze load cases, select the exact required polymer (up to Carbon-Nylon or PC-Blend) and refine surfaces galvanically with real metal when needed. Our goal: Components that withstand industrial rigorousness.

Competencies

Machines and Expertise

FDM Technology

Industrial printers with heated build chambers for warp-free carbon fiber and ABS prints.

SLA Technology

High-resolution resin printing for micro-tolerance fits (0.05mm).

Materials Science

Deep understanding of thermal, mechanical, and chemical load limits of polymers.

Galvanotechnics

In-house plating baths for coating printable plastics with real metal layers.

Design for Additive

Optimization of CAD models for tool-less manufacturing to reduce cost and weight.

Short-Run Logistics

Reliable production and quality control (Bridge-Manufacturing) for up to 500 units.

Workflow

The Path to the Part

01

Analysis

Using our Advisor-Tool or through direct exchange, we define material and technology.

02

CAD & Slicing

Verification of your 3D models. Optimization of orientation and support structures.

03

Printing & QC

Manufacturing on industrial machines followed by dimensional accuracy checks.

04

Refinement

If needed, vapor smoothing or galvanic real-metal coating before Europe-wide shipping.
