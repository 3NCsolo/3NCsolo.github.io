Our Laboratory Portfolio

Thermoplastic Extrusion

High-Performance FDM Printing

We produce stable mechanical components, spare parts, and prototypes on industrial FDM machines. With heated chambers, we can successfully process even difficult polymers without warping.

Our Material Classes:

  • Engineering Plastics: PETG, ABS, ASA (UV-resistant), pure Polycarbonate (PC).
  • High-Tech & Composites: Fiber-reinforced high-performance polymers like PPA-CF, PAHT-CF, PA-GF (Glass fiber) and Carbon-Nylon for extreme mechanical strength.
  • Specialty Polymers: Flexible elastomers (TPU) in various hardness grades, ESD-safe and flame-retardant materials.

Smoothing & Electroplating

A 3D print doesn't have to look like plastic. We offer Vapor Smoothing to seal FDM parts watertight and with a high gloss.

Metal on Plastic:

Our core competence: We coat 3D printed parts (ABS/Resin) in our in-house electroplating facility with various real metals. Perfect for prototypes, EMI shielding, or exclusive individual pieces.
